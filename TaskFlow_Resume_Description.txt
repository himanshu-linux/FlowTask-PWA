**TaskFlow | Full-Stack PWA Productivity Suite**
Architected a high-fidelity productivity platform featuring real-time Firebase cloud synchronization and comprehensive offline support as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

**Key Functionalities**:
- **Real-time Sync**: Instant cross-device task management and secure user authentication.
- **Offline & Installable**: Full PWA capabilities allowing offline usage and home-screen installation.
- **Advanced Management**: Smart search, priority-based filtering, and category organization.
- **Visual Analytics**: Interactive dashboards for tracking productivity trends and weekly completion stats.

**Tech Stack**: React, Firebase (Firestore, Auth), PWA/Workbox, Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS, Recharts.
